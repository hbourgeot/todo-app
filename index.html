How To use?

Fill the Title input, but, the Description input is optional to fill, click on 'Send' And you will see your todo item! Note: you can add 1000 items, no more.

If you want to check any item, just click on the checkbox of the left, and done! you will completed a task, if you want to remove it, click on the button of the right with an 'X' in middle.

For filter your items, click on 'Active', 'Completed', or 'All' buttons, there are located on the bottom of your screen
